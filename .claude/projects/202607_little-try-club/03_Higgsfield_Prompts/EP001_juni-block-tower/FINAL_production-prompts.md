# EP001 최종 제작 프롬프트 — "다시, 블록" (컷2~6)

> 컷1에서 발견된 실수들(오브제 축소, 밋밋한 배경, 헤어컬러 드리프트, 슬로우모션)을
> 전부 반영해 **생성 전에 미리 점검·고정**한 문서. 즉흥적으로 컷마다 다르게 쓰지 않고
> 아래 "고정 블록"을 그대로 재사용한다.

---

## ✅ 생성 전 사전 점검 체크리스트 (매 컷 적용)

- [ ] **눈 색상**: blue (갈색 아님 — `juni-bible.md` 2026.07 정정 반영)
- [ ] **머리 색상**: light sandy-brown (다크 브라운 아님)
- [ ] **오브제 스케일**: 블록 각각 주니 키의 2/3, 명시적으로 "oversized" 기술
- [ ] **오브제 질감**: chunky knitted fabric / felt / yarn stitching (매트 클레이·우드 금지)
- [ ] **배경 3요소 전부 포함**: ① 버터옐로우 벽 ② 그래픽 아트 백드롭 패널 ③ 컨페티 패턴 바닥
- [ ] **텍스트/로고 없음**
- [ ] **(영상만) 속도 지침**: "normal real-time pace, NOT slow motion" + 구체적 초 단위 타이밍 명시. "soft/subtle/gentle motion" 단독 사용 금지
- [ ] **(영상만) 카메라**: Fixed camera, no camera movement (컷5 예외 — 패닝 느낌은 프레이밍 설명으로, 모션 속도 지침은 동일 적용)

## 🔒 고정 스타일 블록 (모든 스틸 프롬프트에 그대로 포함)

```
Clean surreal kids fashion editorial mood. A bold saturated butter-yellow solid studio wall (vivid, not pastel), with a flat graphic art backdrop panel standing behind the character like a photo-studio set piece — abstract overlapping color-block shapes and simple leaf/tulip motifs ONLY in cobalt blue, tomato red, and soft pink, printed flat like a poster — absolutely no numbers, letters, or typography of any kind in the backdrop design. The floor is a colorful confetti-print rug (small scattered dots and shapes in cobalt blue, tomato red, butter yellow, sage green on a cream base). Juni, an 8-10 year old Korean boy character with a soft round face, slightly tousled light sandy-brown hair (matching the reference image exactly — NOT dark brown, NOT black), warm gentle BLUE eyes, coral blush on both cheeks, wearing a cobalt blue knitted vest over a cream-and-cobalt striped long-sleeve top, cobalt blue shorts, cream socks, ivory sneakers, soft matte 3D storybook character style. Maintain his EXACT appearance from the reference image - do not change his face, hair, eye color, or clothing. All blocks: chunky knitted fabric with visible yarn stitching, matte, never shiny; each block roughly two-thirds of Juni's height — this oversized surreal scale is required. No readable text, no logos, no subtitles.
```

## 컷 2 — 탑이 무너진다, 놀람

**진행 상태**: ⬜ 스틸 대기 → ⬜ 영상 대기

### 스틸 프롬프트 (고정 블록 + 아래 씬)
```
Scene: An OVERSIZED tower of 3 giant knitted blocks (cobalt blue, tomato red, butter yellow) toppling and falling sideways, mid-motion, slight motion blur. Juni stands startled, wide eyes, mouth slightly open, one arm still reaching where the tower just was. Fixed eye-level camera with a slight zoom-in, giant blocks filling most of the vertical frame.
```

### 영상 모션 프롬프트 (start_image = 위 스틸)
```
The tower topples and falls sideways, blocks tumbling onto the confetti rug. Juni flinches back with wide startled eyes and a small surprised gasp expression. Natural, normal real-time speed, NOT slow motion — the fall happens quickly and abruptly like a real toppling stack, the whole action completes within about 2 seconds. Fixed camera, no camera movement.
```

```
Scene: 3 giant fallen knitted blocks (cobalt blue, tomato red, butter yellow) scattered on the confetti rug, various tilted angles. Juni crouching small and calm beside them, quietly thoughtful expression — NOT sad, NOT crying, NOT angry. Slow gentle zoom-in framing (camera position only), eye-level to slightly above.
```

### 영상 모션 프롬프트
```
Juni's eyes move gently as he looks over the fallen blocks, then a small natural head tilt of quiet thought. This is a calm, quiet moment but movement itself is at NORMAL real-time speed, NOT slow motion and NOT a dreamy floating quality — just small, genuine, natural gestures like a real kid pausing to think for about 2-3 seconds. Fixed camera, no camera movement.
```
